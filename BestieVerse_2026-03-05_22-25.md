1. Concept

BestieVerse is a blog space created by Bro_Bestie15 dedicated to:

- self-made theories
- thought experiments
- science-inspired ideas
- philosophy + imagination
- “what if” scenarios

It is the idea laboratory of the Bestie blog ecosystem.

2. Purpose

BestieVerse exists to:

- explore ideas beyond normal discussions
- document self-made theories
- combine science curiosity with philosophy
- turn random thoughts into structured concepts

It is not meant to prove scientific facts but to explore possibilities and curiosity.

6. Post Structure

Each BestieVerse post should follow this structure.

1. The Question

Introduce curiosity.

Example:
"What if black holes are connected to white holes?"

---

2. The Known Concept

Explain the real scientific or philosophical idea briefly.

---

3. The Thought Experiment

Introduce the new possibility or theory.

---

4. Exploration

Explain how the idea could work.

---

5. Limitations

Clearly state:

- theoretical nature
- possible contradictions
- current scientific understanding

---

6. Closing Insight

End with a reflective thought.

Example:

"Sometimes curiosity is not about proving truth, but about exploring possibility."

---

7. Writing Principles

BestieVerse posts should follow these rules:

- Curiosity over certainty
- Exploration over conclusion
- Respect science
- Avoid claiming discoveries
- Encourage thinking

---

8. Disclaimer Template

Every theory post should include:

«This post explores speculative ideas inspired by science and philosophy.
These are personal thought experiments and not scientific claims.»
